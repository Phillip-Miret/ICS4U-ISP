Instructions:

1. Add Dye

- Click and drag any part of the canvas to add dye.

2. Place blocks

- On the right (past the line), click and drag the red box
onto the canvas. It will turn blue once it is capable
of interacting with the fluid.

- by clicking and draging any blue box it can either be moved
around on the canvas, or destroyed by moving and dropping
it past the line on the right

3. Placing Source blocks

- By clicking the black square with a dot in the middle,
you can make a source block

- click and drag to the point on the canvas
you want the source block to go

- now click and drag on the canvas to the point
you want the source to flow

- if you hold down the mouse button and stop moving,
a blue line will appear, showing the
relative magnitute, and dricetion the source will be directed

- Release the mouse and a new source will be created
pointing in the same direction the mouse was

- Similarly to the blocks, sources can be moved by clicking
and draging them or destroyed by moving them past the line
